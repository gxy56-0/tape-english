// Getting Started

HK English Typing Practice — 港英打字练习
希望所有在香港生活的港漂，都可以更好地融入这座美丽的城市。 To everyone building a life in Hong Kong — may this tool help you feel at home.
1
Choose your goal   选择目标
Select how many words per round and pick a theme you like.
选择每轮练习多少个单词，挑选你喜欢的主题配色。
2
Start typing   开始打字
See the English word, type it with your keyboard. Upper/lower case both work. Press Backspace to undo.
看到英文单词，直接用键盘输入。大小写随意，退格键可回退。
3
Use the toolbar   使用工具栏
S = sound 音效   W = word list 词库   T = theme 主题   R = review 错词复习
顶栏按钮可切换音效、查看词库、更换主题、复习打错的单词。
4
Finish & review   完成结算
Complete the round to see your WPM, accuracy, and star rating (1-3 stars).
打完一轮查看打字速度(WPM)、正确率和星级评价(1-3星)。
